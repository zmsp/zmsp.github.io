**Triviathon** is a trivia game featuring multiple quiz categories, and competitive leaderboard rankings.

## Key Features

- **Multiple Categories**: Featuring tools to generate custom questions and curiated questions in categories like Tech, History, Science, Pop Culture, and Sports 
- **Timed Challenges**: Test your reflexes and accuracy under pressure.
- **Leaderboards**: Compete against friends and global trivia fans.
