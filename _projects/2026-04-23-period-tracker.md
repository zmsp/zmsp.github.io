I built this minimalist period tracker for friends and family who wanted a 100% private alternative to mainstream apps. There are no accounts, cloud syncing, or data sharing—all data stays locally on your device.

## Screenshots

{% include gallery %}

## Key Features
- **Privacy First**: Your data never leaves your device.
- **Minimalist**: Clean interface focused on your calendar and cycle predictions.
- **Flutter Built**: Works smoothly on both iOS and Android.

## Tech Stack
- **Framework**: Flutter
- **Storage**: Local SQLite persistence
