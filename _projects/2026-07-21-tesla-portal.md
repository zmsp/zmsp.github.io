# Tesla Portal

**Tesla Portal** is a website designed specifically to run smoothly on your Tesla's in-car screen. It brings together everything you need into one clean, easy-to-use dashboard while you are on the road or parked.
![Tesla Theater & AI Assistant Spotlight](/assets/images/projects/tesla-portal-screen-5.webp)
## Key Features

* **Clock & Weather**: Shows the current time and your local weather side-by-side based on your zip code.
* **Find Local Food & Events**: Quickly looks up nearby places to eat and fun activities happening around your current location.
* **Flight Tracker**: Checks real-time flight statuses—perfect for knowing exactly when to pick up friends and family from the airport.
* **Quick Search**: A dedicated tab that lets you search the web quickly right from your dashboard.
* **AI Assistant**: A built-in AI helper ready to answer questions and help you brainstorm ideas on the go.
* **Full-Screen Theater**: Easily launches your favorite web streaming services and forces the Tesla browser to use the entire screen.
* **Arcade Games**: A collection of fun, web-based games to play and pass the time while your car is charging.

## How It's Built

* **Designed for Tesla**: Built using modern web tools optimized to load fast and run reliably inside the car's browser.
* **Smart Saving**: Remember where you left off, even if the car temporarily puts the browser to sleep in the background.
* **Free & Live**: Hosted online and completely free to use. You can access it directly at [https://teslaportal.pages.dev/](https://teslaportal.pages.dev/).
