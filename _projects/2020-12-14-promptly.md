I built **Promptly** because I wanted a clean, distraction-free way to capture video content or give presentations. It’s a simple teleprompter app made with Flutter that handles auto-scrolling and rich text.

## Features
- **Auto-Scrolling**: Adjustable speed to match your natural speaking pace.
- **Rich Text**: Highlight specific parts of your script (like `[Chorus]` for singers).
- **Video Recording**: Record yourself while the text scrolls, so you can focus on the camera.
