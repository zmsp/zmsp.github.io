A versatile, easy-to-use scoreboard application designed for tracking scores across a wide variety of sports and games.

## Features

- **Multi-Sport Support**: Presets for Basketball, Soccer, Volleyball, and more.
- **Customizable Rules**: Adjust game durations, period counts, and scoring increments.
- **Real-Time Clock**: Built-in stopwatch and countdown timers.
- **Persistent History**: Saves match results and statistics locally.
