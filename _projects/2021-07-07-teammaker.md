**Team Maker Buddy** is the smartest way to organize matches, manage rosters, and balance teams based on skill and gender. Built by Referees, for the game.

### Designed for Excellence
Everything you need to manage your sports community in one place.

- **Fair Balancing**: Algorithm-driven team creation based on player skill levels, gender, and availability settings.
- **Roster Management**: Maintain your player database with ease. Add, edit, or remove players with instant local sync.
- **Real-time Tools**: Built-in scoreboard and half-time trackers to keep the game moving professionally.

### Why I built it
I'm a big volleyball fan, but managing teams and rosters at the gym was always a mess. I built **Team Maker Buddy** because I was tired of spending twenty minutes just trying to get a fair game started. It started as a scrappy little tool for my local pickup group, but it’s grown into a professional management app used by coaches, referees, and league organizers around the world.

## Tech Stack
- **Framework**: Flutter
- **Storage**: `shared_preferences`, `localstorage`
- **UI Components**: `data_table_2` for advanced data management.
